Background

Throughout my professional experience I've had a passion for exploring new technologies and a strong entrepreneurial drive that has given me a diverse array of experience in the marketplace.


Experience

Marketing Manager

Early in my career, I led to the expansion of a marketing team at a Brooklyn-based app development startup. Here, I led digital strategy, and helped to lay the groundwork for the company's growth in a competitive market. This job showed me at an early age that my efforts can have a direct impact in the growth of a business. This helped to lead me down the path of creating my own company.

Mobile App Entrepreneur

After this experience I became engrossed in entrepreneurship and was a fellow at the Leslie eLab Entrepreneurship Center at NYU. I taught myself to code and built a mobile app using Swift called Playbook that used RSS technologies to streamline blog reading experience directly from your phone. We saw some early successes and was a top app of the day on Product Hunt.

Cryptocurrency Asset Manager

Continuing my entrepreneurial journey, I eventually became the sole founder of a cryptocurrency hedge fund that managed over 1000 BTC. This venture gave me a lot of experience in navigating emerging markets & technologies. However, this venture also came with significant challenges, my overly eager nature and missteps ended up resulting in the bankruptcy of the fund and my conviction for wire fraud. This period has been a profound learning experience for me and taught me the importance of transparency and business ethics.


Beyond my professional endeavors, I've been actively involved in the tech startup scene. I cultivated a large following on social media where I shared my insights and experiences in the crypto markets. My journey, marked by both achievements and setbacks, reflects my eagerness to explore new markets and technologies, and learn from both my successes and failures.

I am eager to re-enter the marketplace and use my lived experiences to help build and grow new technologies.
